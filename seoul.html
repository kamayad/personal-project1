Background

I got accepted into Korea University as an international transfer student, so I went to Seoul to study for two years. I was supposed to graduate from KU but due to many factors I wasn't able to complete my degree there. I didn't love the school life but I had a really good time living in Seould because of the abundance of places to explore, as well as the vegan cafes and restaurants.

Restaurants

It's surprising how many vagan restaurants there are in Seoul.

Cafes

One of my favorite things to do during the weekends or any time I had time off was going cafe hopping!

My Top Five Vegan Cafes

  1. Bgomi Bakery

    2. Location: Mangwon Station

    Favorite Menu Items:

    • Potato Curry Bread
    • Salt Butter Bread
    • Walnut traditional sweet
    • Grean Tea Latte

    I love the atmosphere in the cafe, although they moved locations since I last visited I'm sure it is as cute as ever! The bears Bgomi and Bbaeggomi are the characters that represent the couple who runs the shop.

  2. Yong Tokki

    3. Location: Kyeonghee University Station

    Favorite Menu Items:

    • Bear Shaped Brioch

      • There are usually around 3-4 flavors available

    • Salt Butter Bread

    Take out only, no steating available. Very cute little shop with a variety of baked goods, the owner is very nice. The bear brioch sell out often, so I reccomend pre-ordering by messaging them on instagram!

  3. Dalyang

    4. Location: Hyehwa/Hansung University Station

    Favorite Menu Items:

    • Soy Milk Soft Serve

      • Choice of cone or cup

    • Tofu Tender Bahn Mi
    • Melon Soda
  4. Achemi
  5. OOH Breado
